Understanding the 4 Cs

C
Cut

The most important factor affecting a diamond's beauty. Cut determines how light travels through the stone and creates that signature sparkle.

C
Color

Graded D (colorless) to Z (light yellow). For most buyers, G–H offers the ideal balance of near-colorlessness and value.

C
Clarity

Measures natural inclusions and blemishes. VS1–VS2 diamonds appear flawless to the naked eye and offer excellent value.

C
Carat

Refers to a diamond's weight, not size. Cut quality affects how large a diamond appears — two stones of the same carat can look very different.
